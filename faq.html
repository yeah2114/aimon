  • [Payment] What form of payment is acceptable?

    - AIMON currently only accepts payment through PayPal. Click the Checkout button and log into PayPal and proceed. If you do not have a PayPal account, simply create a PayPal account, and link a payment method like your bank, credit, and/or debit cards.

  • [Payment] How do I purchase a subscription?

    - Go to the Purchase Membership page through the button at the bottom of the Home Page or under Setting, select the test and subscription, and check out.

  • [Payment] What is your refund policy?

    - Refunds are available for subscription of more than 30 days. Refund amounts are based on the number of days elapsed since the date of the purchase OR the number of questions used, whichever amounts to a higher deductible.

  • [Payment] Is the provided personal information secure?

    - The security of personal information is important to AIMON. The information is securely protected and is not used in any other way, otherwise noted.

  • [Account and Subscription] How can I reset/retrieve my username/password if I forgot them?

    - If you do not remember your password, input your email address under “Forgot password” on the Login Page. A one-time reset link will be sent to your account email.

  • [Account and Subscription] How do I change my profile?

    - You can change/update your profile by click on “Manage Account” under Setting on the right top corner after logging in.

  • [Account and Subscription] When does a newly purchased Study Plan begin?

    - The plan starts right after the purchase.

  • [Account and Subscription] How do I extend a subscription?

    - Simply purchase the same or Combo plan to extend your current plan.

  • [Account and Subscription] Where can I input a promotion code?

    - You can use your promotion code on the Purchase Membership Page.

  • [Technical] What are the device/OS/network requirements?

    - AIMON service is compatible with all Window and Mac computers, Android and IOS based devices. It is recommended, however, to use on devices with screen size greater than 9.7” for the ease of readability.

  • [Technical] What do I do when I run into any technical trouble?

    - Contact us by Contact Us with any technical issue and we will respond as soon as possible.

  • [Contact] How do I report an error in a question?

    - Use the Error Report button on the top right corner of each question.

  • [Contact] Where do I contact for payment/account/system error?

    - Contact us by Contact Us with any technical issue and we will respond as soon as possible.
